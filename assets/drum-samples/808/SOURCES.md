# TR-808 drum samples — provenance & license

These 16 WAVs are recordings of a **Roland TR-808** (serial 103852). DR-1
plays them directly in its sample layer; the original five are also used to
derive the `808*` sample-flavored wavetables via
`scripts/build-drum-tables.mjs`.

- **Clean-Samples catalog:** https://github.com/tidalcycles/Clean-Samples
- **Upstream sample repo:** https://github.com/tidalcycles/sounds-tr808-fischer
- **License:** CC0-1.0 (public domain dedication) — no rights reserved.
- **Source revision:** the upstream repository's `main` branch.
