INSIDE THE ENGINE

Everything a wavetable patch needs, voiced eight ways.

6wavetables
8voices
7unison per osc

20presets

Wavetables you can watch move

Sweep through a table of evolving timbres and hear the tone morph under your fingers, or load your own sample and draw your own waveforms. The 3D view shows exactly where you are in the table, so shaping a sound is something you see as well as hear.

A dual filter with teeth

Two filters you can stack or split across the oscillators, from clean lowpass sweeps to a talking vowel filter and a tuned comb for metallic tones. Push the drive for grit and warmth that stays musical instead of turning to fizz.

  • LP12 / LP24classic lowpass
  • BP · HP · Notchband, high, notch
  • COMBmetallic, tuned to pitch
  • VOWELtalking, A to U

Modulate anything

Envelopes, LFOs and an easy mod matrix let you route movement onto almost anything: the wavetable, the filter, pitch, pan, levels. Make a sound breathe, wobble or evolve across a whole phrase.

Then finish it

A built-in FX rack finishes the patch: drive for warmth, lush chorus, ping-pong delay and reverb for space, and a limiter to keep it loud and clean. Done without leaving the synth.

CLEAN AT EVERY PITCH

Bright up top.
Never brittle.

Plenty of synths turn harsh and grainy on high notes or fast slides. FableSynth stays smooth and clear all the way up, so leads cut through and pads shimmer without that brittle digital edge. Play anywhere on the keyboard and trust what you hear.
