Wavetables you can watch move

Sweep through a table of evolving timbres and hear the tone morph under your fingers. The 3D view shows exactly where you are in the table, so shaping a sound is something you see as well as hear.

Bring your own wavetables

Import any audio file and FableSynth slices it into frames and finds the pitch for you, or draw a single cycle by hand. Either way it's band-limited exactly like the factory tables, so your own waveforms stay clean and never alias — in the browser, in the plugin, and on a DR-1 pad.

A dual filter with teeth

Two filters you can stack or split across the oscillators, from clean lowpass sweeps to a talking vowel filter and a tuned comb for metallic tones. Push the drive for grit and warmth that stays musical instead of turning to fizz.

  • LP12 / LP24classic lowpass
  • BP · HP · Notchband, high, notch
  • COMBmetallic, tuned to pitch
  • VOWELtalking, A to U

Modulate anything

Drag an envelope or LFO straight onto a knob and a colored ring shows the depth — route movement onto the wavetable, the filter, pitch, pan or levels. Make a sound breathe, wobble or evolve across a whole phrase.

Then finish it

A built-in FX rack finishes the patch: drive for warmth, lush chorus, ping-pong delay and reverb for space, and a limiter to keep it loud and clean. Done without leaving the synth.

CLEAN AT EVERY PITCH

Bright up top.
Never brittle.

Plenty of synths turn harsh and grainy on high notes or fast slides. The FableSynth engine stays smooth and clear all the way up — so WT-1 leads cut through, DR-1 hats stay crisp and BL-1 slides never fizz. Play anywhere on the keyboard and trust what you hear.

DR-1 · DRUM MACHINE

Same engine.
Now it hits.

DR-1 puts the WT-1 wavetable engine behind sixteen drum pads. Every kick, snare and hat is synthesized live from morphing wavetables — there isn't a single sample in it — so any sound can be retuned, reshaped or mangled as deep as you like. Drop a WAV on a pad and it becomes a wavetable too.

Sketch grooves in the 16-step sequencer with accents and swing, chain patterns into longer phrases, and keep hats tight with choke groups. Kits save in your browser, and it plays from your computer keyboard or a MIDI pad controller.

BL-1 · ACID BASSLINE

Same engine.
Now it squelches.

BL-1 takes the same wavetable engine and strips it down to a single mono voice built for acid. Program a bassline in the 16-step pitch sequencer, mark steps with accents and slides, and ride the resonant filter while the pattern runs — the classic 303 workflow, powered by morphing wavetables instead of a fixed saw and square.

Because the oscillator is a wavetable, the tone goes places a classic bassline box can't: sweep the table mid-pattern with the bar-locked LFO and the groove moves in time with your song. Patches save in your browser, and it plays from your computer keyboard or a MIDI controller.
