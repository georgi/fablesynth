Wavetables you can watch move

Six procedural tables, sixteen morphable frames each, rendered into nine band-limited mip levels by inverse FFT so high notes never alias. The live 3D terrain shows the exact frame playing, streamed from the DSP thread. Import a sample or draw a cycle and it runs the same band-limit pipeline.

A dual filter with teeth

Two independent zero-delay state-variable filters (the Cytomic topology), plus a tuned comb and an A-E-I-O-U vowel bank. Route them serial, parallel or split per oscillator. The tanh drive is anti-aliased with first-order ADAA, so pushing it on bright material doesn't fold harmonics back down.

  • LP12 / LP24state-variable
  • BP · HP · Notchstate-variable
  • COMBcutoff = pitch, res = feedback
  • VOWELcutoff morphs A→E→I→O→U

Modulate anything

Two ADSR envelopes, two LFOs in five shapes, and a four-slot mod matrix wiring LFOs, the mod envelope, velocity and note straight to wavetable position, filter cutoff and resonance, pitch, amp, pan and oscillator levels.

Then finish it

A real FX rack closes the chain: tanh drive, stereo chorus, a ping-pong delay, reverb and a safety limiter. Per-voice DC blocking keeps every saturation stage honest.

NO ALIASING. MEASURED.

Worst non-harmonic content: −92 dB

Mip transitions are crossfaded inside the 0.475·sr guard band, so glides and pitch bends never step in brightness, the same trick Serum uses. The headless harness verifies it on every build: wavetable correctness, the anti-aliasing floor, every filter type, the full FX chain and all twenty presets.
